# Football Legends Website - Alternative Hosting

## 🚀 Option 1: Vercel (FREE & EASY - Like Netlify)

### Step 1: Go to Vercel
Visit: https://vercel.com

### Step 2: Sign Up/Login
- Click "Sign up" (use GitHub, GitLab, or email)
- Create free account

### Step 3: Deploy Your Site
- Click "Add New..." → "Project"
- Click "Import Git Repository" or "Deploy with GitHub"
- Or click "Deploy" → "Browse" → Select your Football folder
- Upload ALL files:
  - Index.html
  - Messi.html
  - Ronaldo.html

### Step 4: Get Your URL
- Vercel will give you a URL like: `https://football-legends.vercel.app`
- Your site is LIVE instantly!

---

## 🚀 Option 2: GitHub Pages (FREE)

### Step 1: Create GitHub Account
Go to: https://github.com and sign up

### Step 2: Create Repository
- Click "New repository"
- Name: `Football-Legends`
- Make it public
- Click "Create repository"

### Step 3: Upload Files
- Click "Add file" → "Upload files"
- Drag ALL files from your Football folder
- Click "Commit changes"

### Step 4: Enable GitHub Pages
- Go to repository Settings
- Scroll to "Pages" section
- Select "main" branch
- Click Save

### Step 5: Get Your URL
- URL: `https://yourusername.github.io/Football-Legends/`

---

## 🚀 Option 3: Replit (FREE)

### Step 1: Go to Replit
Visit: https://replit.com

### Step 2: Sign Up
Create free account

### Step 3: Create Repl
- Click "Create" → "HTML, CSS, JS"
- Name: Football Legends

### Step 4: Upload Files
- Click "Files" → Upload all your HTML files

### Step 5: Run
- Click "Run" button
- Get your URL from the top
