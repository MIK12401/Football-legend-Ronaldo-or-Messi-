# Football Legends Website

## 🚀 Quick Deploy to Netlify (FREE & EASY)

### Step 1: Go to Netlify
Visit: https://netlify.com

### Step 2: Sign Up/Login
- Click "Sign up" (use GitHub, GitLab, or email)
- Create free account

### Step 3: Deploy Your Site
- Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
- Drag and drop ALL files from your Football folder:
  - Index.html
  - Messi.html  
  - Ronaldo.html

### Step 4: Get Your URL
- Netlify will give you a URL like: `https://amazing-site-name.netlify.app`
- Your site is LIVE instantly!

## 🎯 Your Website Features:
- ⚽ Beautiful landing page
- 🇵🇹 CR7 Ronaldo profile
